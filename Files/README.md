# \uc0\u55356 \u57098  Waveform Video Maker\
\
### Turn Your Music Into Moving Art.\
\
Waveform is a beautiful browser-based music visualizer that transforms audio, images, and animated waveforms into stunning videos.\
\
Upload a song, add an image, customize colors and effects, choose from multiple waveform styles, and export a high-quality 16:9 video\'97all directly in your browser.\
\
---\
\
## \uc0\u10024  Features\
\
* \uc0\u55356 \u57269  Upload MP3, WAV, MP4, and other common audio formats\
* \uc0\u55356 \u57241 \u65039  Record directly from your microphone\
* \uc0\u55357 \u56764 \u65039  Add and resize custom images\
* \uc0\u55356 \u57096  Beautiful color templates and custom color controls\
* \uc0\u55356 \u57256  Background customization\
* \uc0\u55356 \u57098  Multiple animated waveform styles\
* \uc0\u9881 \u65039  Waveform sizing and visualization controls\
* \uc0\u55356 \u57260  Record and export videos as 16:9 MP4 files\
* \uc0\u55357 \u56507  Runs entirely in your browser\
* \uc0\u9889  Fast, lightweight, and easy to use\
\
---\
\
## \uc0\u55357 \u56960  Quick Start\
\
### 1. Music\
\
Upload an audio file or record with your microphone.\
\
### 2. Image\
\
Add an image and position it to create your scene.\
\
### 3. Waveform\
\
Choose a waveform style and adjust its appearance.\
\
### 4. Colors & Backgrounds\
\
Select beautiful template colors or create your own custom palette.\
\
### 5. Record & Export\
\
Record your visualizer and automatically download your video as an MP4.\
\
